FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll?

Click "Student Login", register with your details, and you'll automatically receive a unique registration number.

Are the courses sequential?

Yes. Students must complete each lesson before accessing the next to ensure solid foundational knowledge.

Is there a coding sandbox?

Yes! Our built-in sandbox supports HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Python with real-time preview.

Do I get a certificate?

Students receive a certificate upon completing a course with a passing grade on all assessments and assignments.
